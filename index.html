Let's Introduce Ourselves!

Grade 6: Input/Output Mastery

Key Terms

Identity (الهوية)

Nickname (لقب/اسم الشهرة): A short name used by friends or family.

  • My name is Robert, but my nickname is Rob.
  • Do you have a nickname?
  • Her nickname is "Sunshine" because she is always happy.

Interests (اهتمامات)

Hobby (هواية): An activity you do for fun in your free time.

  • My favorite hobby is playing football.
  • Reading is a great hobby for learning.
  • I need a new hobby for the summer.

Common Phrases

1. "Pleased to meet you." (يسرني لقاؤك)

Context: A polite way to say hello for the first time.

  • Hello, I am Sara. Pleased to meet you.
  • It is a honor. Pleased to meet you too.
  • Pleased to meet you, Mr. Ahmed.

2. "I'm into..." (أنا مهتم بـ)

Context: Used to describe hobbies or interests naturally.

  • I'm into drawing cartoons.
  • Are you into video games?
  • I'm really into science fiction books.

Real-Life Conversations

Omar: Hi! I don't think we've met. I'm Omar.
Ali: Hey Omar! I'm Ali. Are you new here?
Omar: Yes, I just joined the 6th grade today.
Ali: Welcome! Where are you from originally?
Omar: I am from Meknes. It's a beautiful city.
Ali: Cool! I hope you like our school.
Lina: Excuse me, is this seat taken?
Mona: No, go ahead. I'm Mona, by the way.
Lina: Thanks, Mona. I'm Lina. Nice to meet you.
Mona: Nice to meet you too. What's your favorite subject?
Lina: I'm really into English and Art.
Mona: Me too! We should study together.

Avoid These Errors

I am have 12 years old.

I am 12 years old.

Why: In English, we use the verb "to be" for age, not "to have".

Nice to meet your.

Nice to meet you.

Why: "Your" is possessive. You need the object pronoun "you".
