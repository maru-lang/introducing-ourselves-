Vocabulary

Basic Terms

Name (اسم) - The word by which a person is known.

  • My name is Sarah.
  • What is your name?
  • He wrote his name on the paper.

Age (عمر) - How old someone is.

  • I am 12 years old.
  • She asked about his age.
  • At the age of 10, he moved to a new city.

Hobby (هواية) - An activity done for enjoyment.

  • My hobby is drawing.
  • He has many hobbies.
  • Reading is her favorite hobby.

Expressions & Usage

"Hello, my name is..." (مرحبا، اسمي...) - Use when meeting someone new.

  • Hello, my name is Ali.
  • Hello, my name is Sara.
  • Hello, my name is Omar.

"Nice to meet you" (سعيد بلقائك) - Use after introductions.

  • Nice to meet you, Fatima.
  • Nice to meet you, teacher.
  • Nice to meet you, everyone.

Real-Life Dialogues

Ali: Hello, my name is Ali.
Sara: Hi Ali, I’m Sara.
Ali: Nice to meet you.
Sara: Nice to meet you too.
Ali: How old are you?
Sara: I’m 12 years old.

Common Mistakes

I am 12 years old years.
I am 12 years old.
Explanation: "years" is repeated unnecessarily.
