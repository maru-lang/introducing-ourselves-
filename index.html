Introducing Ourselves - Grade 6 ESL

Built with the 80/20 Rule: focus on the most useful words and phrases first.
Uses the Input/Output Method: learn → notice → practice → speak → write.

Key Vocabulary

Basic Identity

Name (الاسم)

The word people use to call you.

  • My name is Sara.
  • What is your name?
  • His name is Ali.
Age (العمر)

How old someone is.

  • I am eleven years old.
  • What is your age?
  • Her age is twelve.

School Life

Student (تلميذ)

A person who learns at school.

  • I am a student.
  • The student is friendly.
  • We are students.
Grade (المستوى الدراسي)

Your school level or class year.

  • I am in grade 6.
  • She is in my grade.
  • Our grade is fun.

Interests

Hobby (هواية)

An activity you enjoy.

  • My hobby is drawing.
  • Football is his hobby.
  • I have many hobbies.
Favorite (المفضل)

The thing you like most.

  • Blue is my favorite color.
  • Pizza is her favorite food.
  • What is your favorite game?

Expressions & Usage

Hello, my name is... (مرحبا، اسمي...)

Use when meeting someone.

  • Hello, my name is Omar.
  • Hello, my name is Lina.
  • Hello, my name is Sam.
Nice to meet you. (سعيد بلقائك)

Use after meeting a person.

  • Nice to meet you, Ali.
  • Nice to meet you too.
  • It is nice to meet you.
How old are you? (كم عمرك؟)

Ask age politely.

  • How old are you?
  • I am eleven.
  • She asked, how old are you?
I am from... (أنا من...)

Talk about country/city.

  • I am from Morocco.
  • I am from Fes.
  • He is from Spain.
I like... (أنا أحب...)

Talk about interests.

  • I like football.
  • I like reading.
  • I like music.
What is your favorite...? (ما هو ... المفضل لديك؟)
  • What is your favorite color?
  • What is your favorite sport?
  • What is your favorite food?
I am in grade 6. (أنا في المستوى السادس)
  • I am in grade 6.
  • My brother is in grade 6.
  • Are you in grade 6?
See you later. (أراك لاحقاً)
  • See you later, Sara.
  • Okay, see you later.
  • See you later at school.

Real-Life Dialogues

Yara: Hello, my name is Yara.
Adam: Hi, I am Adam.
Yara: Nice to meet you.
Adam: Nice to meet you too.
Yara: How old are you?
Adam: I am eleven.

Omar: Where are you from?
Sara: I am from Fes.
Omar: Great! I am from Rabat.
Sara: Nice city!
Omar: Thank you.
Sara: You are welcome.

Lina: What is your hobby?
Noah: I like drawing.
Lina: Nice! I like reading.
Noah: What is your favorite book?
Lina: Animal stories.
Noah: Cool!

Teacher: What grade are you in?
Huda: I am in grade 6.
Teacher: Very good.
Huda: Thank you.
Teacher: Welcome to class.
Huda: Thank you, teacher.

Common Mistakes

My name Ali.
Right: My name is Ali.
Need the verb is.
I am from Morocco?
Right: I am from Morocco.
Use a period, not a question mark.
How old you are?
Right: How old are you?
Correct question order.
I likes football.
Right: I like football.
Use like with I.
I am in grade six years old.
Right: I am in grade 6.
Grade and age are different ideas.

Image Practice & Open Questions

Part 1: Look & Describe

What do you see? I see a ...

I see a boy. He is saying hello.

What do you see? This is ...

This is a school bus.

What do you see? I see two ...

I see two friends. They are talking.

Part 2: Open-Ended Questions

1. What is your name and how old are you?
My name is Youssef and I am eleven years old.
2. Where are you from?
I am from Morocco. I live in Fes.
3. What is your favorite hobby?
My favorite hobby is football.
4. What do you like at school?
I like English and art class.
5. Why is it good to introduce yourself?
It helps me make friends and speak politely.
